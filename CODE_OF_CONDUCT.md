# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants are expected to keep collaboration respectful, technical, and
focused on the work. Harassment, threats, personal attacks, and discriminatory
conduct are not acceptable.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions,
review comments, community chat, and project events.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or ban
participants who violate this code. Enforcement should be proportional,
documented when appropriate, and focused on keeping the project usable for
contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the project's security or maintainer contact
channels once they are published. Until then, open a private maintainer contact
path before broad community launch.
